Metric Definitions
Slip
A decision that was approved by the engine but later found to be problematic. Indicates false negatives in the model.
Correction
A decision that was rejected or flagged for review by the engine but later found to be acceptable. Indicates false positives.
Human Override
Any decision where a human reviewer changed the engine's original recommendation. Tracks human-in-the-loop activity.
Review Rate
Percentage of total decisions that were flagged for human review. Calculated as: (review decisions / total decisions) * 100.
Drift
Change in input feature distributions over time compared to training data. Monitored for credit score, income, and DTI ratio.
